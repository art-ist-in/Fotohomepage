# find out more about my life as a photographer

Back in the old days, at the innocent age of 14, I sold my first analog photographs. While I was comparing each print separately with the negatives in order to match them to the correct order, I was silently wondering what I had brought upon me... a lifelong passion.
I have never stopped photographing since, studying Fine Arts along the way and travelling, learning to see and to compose, to explore the right moment and align technology, place and feeling. 
I have published in print and exhibted my works, a selection of which you now find online. Enjoy.
